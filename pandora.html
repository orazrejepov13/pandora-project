PANDORA

Pandora is a Danish jewellery manufacturer and retailer founded in 1982 by Per Enevoldsen
Company started as a family-run jewellery shop in Copenhagen
Pandora is known for its customizable charm bracelets, designer rings, earrings, necklaces and (now discontinued) watches
The company has two production sites in Thailand and markets its products in more than 100 countries on six continents with more than 6,700 points of sale
